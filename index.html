Our Objective

To empower girls and young women in Africa by providing access to high-demand tech skills, mentoring, and career pathways that enable them to thrive and compete in the global digital economy.

Mission

To bridge the gender gap in tech by cultivating a safe, inclusive, and transformative learning space that prepares African girls for impactful careers in Software Engineering and Cybersecurity

Vision

A world where girls are not just participants but leaders in the global tech revolution—writing the future, securely and confidently.

What you can do with us

Sponsorship
We welcome sponsorships with organizations and institutions that share our vision of empowering young girls in tech. Together, we can amplify impact, expand our reach, and foster long-term sustainability.
Partnership
Your partnership helps us provide free, high-quality tech training to underserved girls. Join us in removing financial barriers and investing in the next generation of female innovators and cybersecurity experts.
Collaboration
GISEC thrives on meaningful collaborations with tech hubs, NGOs, educational bodies, and change-makers. Let’s co-create programs, events, and solutions that drive digital inclusion for girls across Africa and beyond.
Mentorship
Mentors are the backbone of GISEC. By sharing your expertise, guidance, and encouragement, you can shape a girl’s journey from curiosity to confidence and competence in tech.
Funding Opportunities
We continuously seek local and international funding opportunities to support our mission. Whether through grants, donations, or CSR initiatives, your support fuels our impact and growth.
